"Book Maniac" is a product obtained by merging online stores that set up a retail setting with a platform that lets contribution possible, resulting in an efficient solution of reducing book wastage and a profitable aspect that sustains itself. Our site also aims at providing a library to showcase all the books available from our site. The site is also an easy key to all the readers who practice the circulation of used books in real life wherein they also get a virtual platform to practice the same with a wider group of readers.
